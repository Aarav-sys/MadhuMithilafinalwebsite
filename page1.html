About Madhubani 

Madhubani is an art form which has an origin from Bihar, India and is popular worldwide in other countries, like Japan as well. 
The artworks are made by the castes of a house, and it is a culture that everyone should be adopting this form of art. 
There are 3 types of Kohbar in the paintings. The Kohbar's are made during the weddings and these are made by Brahmin and Kayastha women. The elements and color which are used are Earth-Yellow, Water- White, Fire-Red, Sky-Blue, and Air-Black
