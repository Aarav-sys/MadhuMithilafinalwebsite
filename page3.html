1914-2005
Sita Devi

She was born in a village near Saharsa, which is located in the state of Bihar.
Sita Devi learnt how to paint and she was the first artist to practice the belief of painting murals with Madhubani art, and due to her interest in arts, she began to sell her arts.
Sita was a local artist, and then she brought attention to everyone, which caused her to be a national level artist.

1968-
Dulari Devi

Dulari Devi was born in the village of Ranti, which is located in the state of Bihar, India.
She was an illiterate person, since she did not go to school, and she did not study, so she took up arts and that was the day she entered the world of arts
The real journey of Dulari Devi began when the master of Madhubni arts, Mahasundari Devi, introduced her to Karpoori Devi, who was also a budding artist of Madhubani. Karpoori Devi taught Dulari the main strokes and the paints which should be used in Madhubani art

15 April 1922-4 July 2013
Mahasundari Devi

She was born on 15th April 2022, and was barely literate. She learnt all about Madhubani artwork after leaving the purdah system and producing her interest in arts.
She found a cooperative society, which was named Mithila Hastashilp Kalakar Audyogik Sahyog Samiti, which promoted all the artists of Madhubani to make handicrafts and boost growth and development. She was known for her expertise in clay, paper mache, sujani and Sikki.
She passed away on 4th July 2013, in a private hospital at the age of 92 

1940-
Baua Devi

She was born in 1940, in the village of Jitwarpur. Her village was located in the Madhubani district of Bihar. 
She has been practicing this form of art for 60 years, and she has been following this career for a long time.
Her artworks have been widely known in Spain , France and Japan

1928-1991
Ganga Devi

She was born in 1928, in the city of Mithila, which is located in the state of Bihar. Since her family was into arts, she also took up arts. She became fond of arts when she was handed a brush by her mom, and that brush was made from rice straws and a few threads drawn from the hem of a sari.

Mahalaxmi

She was born in the village of Ranti, which was located in the State of Bihar, but was nearby the borders of Nepal. 
She got the training to make Madhubani artworks under the guidance of his teacher and estemeed painting Santosh Kumar Das.
She makes half of the artwork using acrylic paint on paper, and she makes half of the artworks on canvas and murals as well.  She gets her subjects from Hindu poems, and she breaks the poem into different artworks 

1984-
Malvika Raj

She was born in the city of Patna, which is located in Bihar. She learnt art from an artist named Ashok Biswas.
The main learnings she gained from making Madhubani art were making motifs, and she learnt more about Dalit-women motifs in Samastipur, Patna.
