Problems faced by the artists 

During the visit to Madhubani, we have met with different artists and got many inputs about how they created their artwork and sell it. 
During the visit, one of the artists expresses that whenever they sell their artworks, they sell it through a Mumbai-based company and whenever their artwork is sold, they earn profit. 
Some of the problems faced by the artist are the cost of buying a canvas. One of the artists explained that whenever they have to go buy a canvas, it costs Rs.500/-, which costs them a lot of money, and in result, they have to use their hard earned cash. 
Secondly, one of artist also told that whenever they have to sell their artworks, the buyer always claim that the artwork is theirs. 

How did technology shape art

In this section, learn how did different uses of technology help in arts

Use of Microsoft Paint

The first application where artists can draw digitally is the Microsoft Paint app, in which there are different types of brushes and pencils which can be used to produce different form of arts. 

Use of Krita 

Krita is a brand new application in which artists can now choose different sizes in which they would like to produce their artwork and also, there is a variety of brushes which can be used, and also, changing color is easy. 
I have taught the artists to use this platform in the laptop, but they preferred to draw their digital artworks on mobile 

Use of Sketchbook

Due to the availability of artists drawing digitally, Sketchbook is a device which the artists can use to draw their artworks digitally. 
The artists learned how to draw digitally, using this app 

Teaching artists how to draw digitally 

With the availability and the ease of using sketchbook, the artists are now able to draw digitally. 
One of the artists, Narmada Devi, was able to make 2 artists after teaching the required basics, and then, I thought that every artist will now be able to draw digitally. Also, another artists was able to draw digitally after learning the required basics.
