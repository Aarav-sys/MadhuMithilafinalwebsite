About Madhu Mithila

Madhu Mithila is a driven foundation, which aims to help the underprivileged artists to draw digitally, in the art form of Madhu Mithila. 
This is a foundation which is joining hands with Bihar Government driven foundation, Jeevika, or BRLPS

About Jeevika 

Jeevika is a government driven foundation, run by the government of Bihar, since it is focusing on providing self employment opportunities to the underprivileged people. 
Currently, since we have met with the Chief operating officer of Jeevika, this project has been in assosciation with the Jeevika foundation
